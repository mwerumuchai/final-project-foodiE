Rice and Beef Recipe

Prep: 30Mins Cook: 1hr 10Mins
Ready in: 1hr 40Min

Ingredients For rice

  • 6 cups water, divided
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice

Method

  • In a saucepan with a good fitting lid bring water, salt and butter if desired to a boil.
  • Add rice and stir.
  • Cover and reduce heat to medium low. You will know that your temperature is correct if a little steam is visible leaking from the lid. A lot of steam means your heat is too high.
  • Cook for 20 minutes. DO NOT LIFT LID!
  • The steam that is trapped inside the pan is what allows the rice to cook properly.
  • Remove from heat and fluff with fork.

Ingredients For Beef Stew

  • Beef ( cut into cubes)
  • 2 zucchini ( cut into strips)
  • 1 large Onion diced
  • 2 cloves of Garlic Minced
  • 1 capsicum shredded
  • Water or beef stock
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander
  • Black Pepper
  • Oil to fry
  • Other seasoning that you like

Method

  • Cook onions until brown, then put meat/beef.
  • Cover to cook until the water produced by the meat starts to disappear.Add the zucchini, tomatoes and capsicum and cover.
  • After a few minutes add some beef stock or water (whichever you have), salt, pepper, minced garlic and any other seasoning and cover to cook.
