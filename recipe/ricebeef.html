Rice Beef Stew Recipe

Prep: 30Mins Cook: 1hr 10Mins Ready in: 1hr 40Min

Ingredients For rice

  1. 6 cups water, divided
  2. 2 teaspoons salt
  3. 1 cup long-grain white rice

Method

  1. In a saucepan with a good fitting lid bring water, salt and butter if desired to a boil.
  2. Add rice and stir.
  3. Cover and reduce heat to medium low. You will know that your temperature is correct if a little steam is visible leaking from the lid. A lot of steam means your heat is too high.
  4. Cook for 20 minutes.

    5. DO NOT LIFT LID!

    The steam that is trapped inside the pan is what allows the rice to cook properly.

  5. Remove from heat and fluff with fork.

Ingredients For Beef Stew

  1. Beef ( cut into cubes)
  2. 2 zucchini ( cut into strips)
  3. 1 large Onion diced
  4. 2 cloves of Garlic Minced
  5. 1 capsicum shredded
  6. Water or beef stock
  7. Salt to taste
  8. Fresh Coriander
  9. Black Pepper
  10. Oil to fry

    11. Other seasoning that you like

Method

  1. Cook onions until brown, then put meat/beef.
  2. Cover to cook until the water produced by the meat starts to disappear.Add the zucchini, tomatoes and capsicum and cover.
  3. After a few minutes add some beef stock or water (whichever you have), salt, pepper, minced garlic and any other seasoning and cover to cook.
