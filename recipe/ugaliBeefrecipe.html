Ugali Beef Stew Recipe

Prep: 15Mins Cook: 1hr 10Mins Ready in: 1hr 25Min

Ingredients For Ugali

  1. Maize Flour
  2. Water

    3. Mwiko ( A wooden cooking stick with a flat end).

Method

  1. Put water to boil. Once it has boiled start adding the maize flour turning until it starts to thicken. Continue stirring because if you stop lumps will form and your ugali wonâ€™t taste good.
  2. Once you attain your desired thickness (this depends on how hard you like your ugali) continue turning it with a mwiko (wooden spoon or stick) giving each side sometime to cook.
  3. Cooking ugali usually takes 10-15 minutes. Once your ugali is done turn it over into a hot pot and keep warm until ready to serve!

Ingredients For Beef Stew

  1. Beef ( cut into cubes)
  2. 2 zucchini ( cut into strips)
  3. 1 large Onion diced
  4. 2 cloves of Garlic Minced
  5. 1 capsicum shredded
  6. Water or beef stock
  7. Salt to taste
  8. Fresh Coriander
  9. Black Pepper
  10. Oil to fry

    11. Other seasoning that you like

Method

  1. Cook onions until brown, then put meat/beef.
  2. Cover to cook until the water produced by the meat starts to disappear.Add the zucchini, tomatoes and capsicum and cover.
  3. After a few minutes add some beef stock or water (whichever you have), salt, pepper, minced garlic and any other seasoning and cover to cook.
